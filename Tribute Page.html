Lana Del Rey

Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, born 21st June 1985, in Manhattan, New York.

Lana Del Rey, American singer and songwriter, known for her melancholic style and vintage Hollywood aesthetic.

Here's a timeline of Lana Del Rey's life:

  • 1985 - Born Manhattan, New York.
  • 2004 - She enrolled at Fordham University where she majored in philosophy, with an emphasis on metaphysics.
  • 2005 - Moved to New York City to pursue a music career.
  • 2010 - She released her self-titled debut studio album, Lana Del Ray aka Lizzy Grant.
  • 2011 - Her breakthrough came with the viral success of her single Video Games.
  • 2012 - She released her second album Born to Die which became a number-one album in the UK.
  • 2014 - She released her third album Ultraviolence.
  • 2015 - She released her fourth album Honeymoon.
  • 2017 - She released her fifth album Lust for Life.
  • 2019 - She released her sixth album Norman Fucking Rockwell! which was named one of the 500 Greatest Albums of All Time by Rolling Stone.
  • 2021 - She released her seventh and eighth albums Chemtrails over the Country Club and Blue Banisters.
  • 2023 - She released her ninth album Did You Know That There's a Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd.
  • 2024 - She headlined at Leeds Festival and announced her UK and Ireland Tour 2025.
